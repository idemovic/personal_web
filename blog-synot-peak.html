Synot Peak Case Study: Elevating ELIA’s Product Strategy

Published on 2026-03-13

Synot Peak was a learning loop for transforming product ideas into scalable outcomes. This post walks through the discovery, strategy, execution, and measured impact across the organization.

Challenge

Align product discovery, development, and go-to-market to deliver quick customer value.

Approach

Discovery, lean roadmap, cross-functional collaboration, and defined success metrics.

Outcomes

Faster onboarding, stronger product-market fit signals, and a repeatable playbook for PLG at ELIA.

Solution highlights

  • High-concurrency retention engine with multi-tenant support and real-time analytics.
  • Modular Compliance Layer to toggle features by jurisdiction without core changes.
  • Real-Time Signal Architecture for thousands of concurrent events.
  • In-game overlay delivering a seamless retention experience.

Architecture & Engineering

Event-driven architecture, in-game widgets, automatic payouts, and real-time analytics integrated into a unified platform.

Outcomes & Business Impact

100% proprietary ownership, market-ready speed (12 months), and a scalable playbook for future promotions and tenants.

Quoted: "Thanks to ELIA, we now have a high‑performance engagement and promotion platform that significantly elevates our capabilities. We look forward to continuing collaboration."
